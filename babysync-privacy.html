Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 3, 2026

Overview

BabySync+ is designed with your privacy as a top priority. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. Your baby's data stays entirely on your device and in your private iCloud account.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. BabySync+ does not require account creation, email registration, or any form of sign-up. The app functions entirely offline and locally on your device.

Data Storage

All data you enter (feeding logs, diaper records, sleep times, growth measurements) is stored:

  • Locally on your iPhone using Apple's SwiftData framework
  • In your private iCloud via Apple's CloudKit, if iCloud is enabled on your device

We have no access to your iCloud data. Apple manages the encryption and security of your iCloud storage.

Third-Party Services

BabySync+ does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no ads in the app.

Widgets, Siri & App Intents

BabySync+ offers Home Screen widgets and Siri voice commands for convenience. These features use Apple's on-device frameworks (WidgetKit, AppIntents) and do not send data to any external server. Data is shared between the app and widgets through a local App Group container on your device.

Children's Privacy

BabySync+ is a parenting tool used by adults to track their baby's activities. The app does not collect any information directly from children. All data entry is performed by the parent or caregiver.

Data Deletion

You can delete any individual record within the app at any time. To remove all data, simply delete the app from your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, you can also remove the data from iCloud via Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
