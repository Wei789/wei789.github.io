Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add the widget to my Home Screen?

Long press on your Home Screen, tap the "+" button in the top-left corner, search for "BabySync", and choose the Small or Medium widget size.

How do I use Siri to log activities?

You can say commands like:

  • "Log bottle feeding BabySync"
  • "Log diaper change BabySync"
  • "Start sleep BabySync"

You can also find and customize these shortcuts in the Shortcuts app.

Does my data sync between devices?

Yes. If you have iCloud enabled, your data automatically syncs across all your devices signed in with the same Apple ID. No setup required.

Do I need to create an account?

No. BabySync+ works immediately with no sign-up, no email, and no account creation.

How do I set up feeding reminders?

Go to Settings (gear icon) > Notification Settings > enable "Feeding Reminder" and choose an interval from 1 to 6 hours. The widget will show a countdown to the next feeding.

How do I view growth charts?

Go to Settings > Growth Chart. You'll need to set up a baby profile first (name, birth date, gender). Then you can add weight and height records to see them plotted against WHO percentile curves.

How do I delete a record?

On the Home Screen, swipe left on any activity in the Recent Records list and tap Delete. You can also tap any record to view its details and make edits.

How do I delete all my data?

Delete the app from your device. To also remove iCloud data, go to iOS Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage and remove BabySync+ data.
